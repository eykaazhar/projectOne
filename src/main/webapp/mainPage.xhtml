FACILITIES

House

A common UK house with 2 bedrooms, toilet, living room and kitchen. Each room is installed with environmental monitoring equipment and video cameras

Experimental Garden

A small garden located beside the house available for gardening and outside activities

Control Room

A room located beside the house that stored devices and equipment to manage the environmental monitoring equipment and video cameras. Need a special key for access.
